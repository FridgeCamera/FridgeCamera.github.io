Fridge Camera

Fridge Camera is our answer to a smart fridge: a compact and zero-fuss device that can transform any standard, no-frills fridge into a money and time saving, waste-busting powerhouse. How many times have you forgotten to write a shopping list and found yourself wandering around the supermarket throwing products into your basket only to find you already have them at home? The Fridge Camera solves this problem with our mobile app.

Our wireless fridge camera has been designed to help reduce this food waste and save money whilst also helping the environment. There is no need to replace your current fridge, Fridge Camera can be added into any fridge to transform it into a smart fridge.

Mobile App

Organize Your Fridge

Organizes all of the refrigerator contents into a stock tab that showcases the items, amount of items remaining, and expiration dates.

Meal Suggestions

Suggests meals based on the current contents in the fridge and provide recipes and instructions to make these meals.

Self-updating Lists

Access shopping lists where items are automatically added based on low or expired products.

Shared Shopping Lists

Allows user to create groups with shared stocks and shopping lists which is desirable for groups living together such as college students.
